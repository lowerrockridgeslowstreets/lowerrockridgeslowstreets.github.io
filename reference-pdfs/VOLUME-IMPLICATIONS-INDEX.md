## 1. `01-JCC-East-Bay-Project-CEQA-Analysis.pdf` — **new trips / burden (not a neighborhood “volume reduction” win)**

**What it actually quantifies:** *Net new peak-hour vehicle trips* from the project, plus Oakland TDM/VTR policy thresholds.

- **Non-summer typical day:** 195 net new a.m. peak hour vehicle trips; 184 net new p.m. peak hour vehicle trips. (~12–13% of trips from site staff, rest from visitors.)  
  **(PDF p. 48, “According to the Jewish Community Campus… Fehr & Peers… September 2024”.)**

- **Summer (summer camp in session):** *Up to* 338 net new a.m. peak hour vehicle trips; 118 net new p.m. peak hour vehicle trips.  
  **(Same page — the 338 figure is **a.m. / summer scenario**, not a single “338 peak” without context.)**

- **Mitigation framing:** City SCAs require TDM; projects with 100+ net new peak hour trips must target **20% vehicle trip reduction (VTR)** from the TDM plan. **(p. 48–49, Table 5 area.)**

**Implication for copy:** Use this PDF for “**added load** to the street network if unmitigated” and “**CEQA + trip caps / TDM**,” not for “this facility reduced cut-through by X%.”

## 4. `04-Oakland-81st-Ave-demonstration-project-summary-2024-12-17.pdf` — **pilot: large volume drops on treated legs + spillover to 82nd**

**Slideshow summary of the 3-week Oct–Nov 2024 pilot** (Boudart, Corona, Mei; OakDOT).

- **Plymouth St:** e.g. **65–75%** decrease northbound; **25–51%** southbound (narrative “Meeting our Goals” slide).

- **81st Ave:** e.g. **~70%** eastbound, **~35%** westbound (same section).

- **Spillover / displacement (volume *increase* elsewhere):** **22–28%** increase on **82nd Ave** (with extra daily vehicles stated on the outcomes slide); text explains this as some cut-through **shifting to an alternate local route** rather than only disappearing from the region.

- **85th %ile speed:** e.g. **4.3 mph** drop westbound in one segment in front of the school; other segments “negligible.”

- **Narrative quote:** “Traffic filter **reduced volumes by over 70%** on 81st St in the **northbound** direction and on **Plymouth in the eastbound** direction,” with **&lt;30%** increase on 82nd in both directions (summary slide).

**Implication:** Strong **Oakland-owned** before/after for a **diverter + one-way** pilot; any site copy should **name the stress period (3-week pilot, East Oakland, Pride Elementary area)** and **acknowledge 82nd** if you discuss “volume implications” honestly.
